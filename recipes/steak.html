Pan fried steak

this recipe will help you if you are looking to make a delicious steak at home without a fancy grill.

ingredients:

  • steak cut(any kind)
  • oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • butter
  • thyme
  • feel free to add any seasonings you like

Steps:

  1. season the steak
  2. coat a cast iron pan with oil and heat up
  3. set the steak in the pan
  4. cook the steak for about 1 minute on each side
  5. add butter to the pan and the thyme
  6. keep cooking flipping every minute or so until desired rareness
  7. be sure to let rest for at least 3 minutes after cooking
