simple mac and cheese

everyone should know how to make a good bowl of mac and cheese but if you don't know already here is a recipe

ingredients:

  • macaroni
  • cheese
  • butter
  • milk

steps:

  1. boil a pot of water on the stove
  2. add macaroni to boiling water and cook
  3. strain the noodles and add back to the pot
  4. add cheese,milk, and butter and mix up>
  5. you should now have a very simple pot of mac and cheese
