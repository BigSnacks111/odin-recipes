spaghetti

spaghetti is a very yummy food that almost everyone can enjoy. it is very easy to make and everyone can make it.

ingredients:

  • spaghetti noodles
  • spaghetti sauce (store bought or homemade)
  • ground beef
  • seasonings of choice

steps:

  1. boil water
  2. cook the noodles
  3. strain the noodles
  4. brown meat
  5. strain meat>
  6. mix noodles,meat,sauce,and seasonings all in the pot
